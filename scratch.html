The Do's and Don'ts of Diving

  1. Bring Proper Equipment
      2. Diving mask
      Diving fins
      weights + weight belt (if applicable)

  2. Proper Technique
      3. Slow your heart rate down before diving
      Calm yourself down, body and mind
      Never have a snorkel in your mouth when diving below the surface
      Long and smooth movements (almost slow) when diving

  3. Never Dive Alone
      4. If you shallow water blackout, and no one is near, 95% of the time, you will die.
      It makes it more enjoyable!
      Someone to drive the boat or help hold equipment.
